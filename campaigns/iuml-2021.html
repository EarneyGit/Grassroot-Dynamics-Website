Winning in the Face of Statewide Headwinds

Year: 2021 Region: 27 Constituencies in Kerala

Summary: Comprehensive constituency-level operations that overcame adverse statewide political conditions.

← Back to Portfolio

Role & Contribution

Led comprehensive campaign strategy across 27 IUML-backed constituencies during Kerala's historic 2021 Assembly Elections. Our team focused on hyperlocal issues and social media outreach to younger voters while navigating challenging anti-UDF sentiment statewide.

Electoral Context

The 2021 Kerala Assembly Elections were unprecedented, with LDF seeking historic re-election against UDF. IUML faced unique challenges as anti-incumbency sentiment against UDF nationally conflicted with local dynamics. The campaign required delicate balance between state and constituency-level messaging.

Strategic Actions

  • Developed constituency-specific strategies for all 27 IUML constituencies
  • Implemented hyperlocal issue-based campaigning to overcome state-level headwinds
  • Launched targeted social media outreach campaigns for younger demographic groups
  • Created differentiated messaging that separated local performance from UDF brand challenges
  • Focused on micro-level voter mobilization and community engagement
