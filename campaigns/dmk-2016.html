Role & Contribution

DMK partnered with OMG Group for comprehensive ground intelligence operations across Tamil Nadu's South Zone. Our team conducted extensive voter surveys and sentiment analysis to provide objective insights that challenged prevailing campaign assumptions.

Electoral Context

The 2016 Tamil Nadu Assembly Elections were held in the shadow of Chief Minister J. Jayalalithaa's charismatic leadership and AIADMK's incumbency. While most predictions favored a close contest, ground realities suggested different voter dynamics that traditional polling was missing.

Strategic Actions

  • Conducted early-stage ground research and voter sentiment analysis across South Zone constituencies
  • Identified early warning signs contradicting internal optimistic projections
  • Tracked women voter preferences, revealing underestimated AIADMK support
  • Monitored regional voter turnout patterns and constituency-specific issues
  • Provided objective data analysis free from campaign bias
