Role & Contribution

Provided comprehensive intelligence and voter profiling services for IUML campaigns in Kerala (Malappuram & Ponnani) and IJK Party campaigns in Tamil Nadu (Perambalur & Kallakurichi). Our multi-state approach delivered crucial voter sentiment analysis and demographic profiling for strategic campaign positioning.

Electoral Context

The 2024 Lok Sabha Elections presented unique challenges with simultaneous campaigns across two southern states. IUML sought to maintain its Kerala strongholds while IJK Party needed strategic clarity for Tamil Nadu's complex political landscape. Early field surveys were critical for pre-poll positioning in both states.

Strategic Actions

  • Conducted strategic ground surveys in Malappuram and Ponnani constituencies (Kerala)
  • Delivered comprehensive demographic profiling for Perambalur and Kallakurichi (Tamil Nadu)
  • Analyzed voter sentiment patterns and issue priorities across different regions
  • Shaped constituency-specific messaging and campaign operations optimization
  • Provided early field intelligence for pre-poll strategic positioning
