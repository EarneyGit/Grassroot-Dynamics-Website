Role & Contribution

Partnered with Design Box to provide comprehensive grassroots strategy and booth-level planning for INC's Karnataka campaign. Our team developed real-time operational plans, improved field feedback systems, and implemented localized messaging across diverse constituencies to enhance campaign efficiency and voter turnout.

Electoral Context

The 2023 Karnataka Assembly Elections presented INC with a critical opportunity to rebuild its presence in a key southern state. After years in opposition, the party needed to demonstrate renewed organizational strength and connect with voters across Karnataka's diverse linguistic, cultural, and economic landscape.

Strategic Actions

  • Developed comprehensive booth-level planning and volunteer management systems
  • Created real-time operational plans that adapted to changing campaign dynamics
  • Implemented robust field feedback systems to monitor ground-level sentiment
  • Designed localized messaging strategies for Karnataka's diverse constituencies
  • Enhanced resource efficiency and voter turnout through improved coordination
